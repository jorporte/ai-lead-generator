Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Rebel Wheels Lead Generator is an internal automation tool used by Rebel Wheels and Tires to identify tire deals and publish promotional content to business-owned social media accounts.

Information We Access

When connected to Meta services, this application may access basic Facebook Page and Instagram professional account information needed to publish content to accounts managed by Rebel Wheels and Tires. This may include Page identifiers, Instagram business account identifiers, access tokens, and publishing permissions granted by an authorized administrator.

How We Use Information

We use the information only to create and publish promotional tire-deal posts to connected Rebel Wheels and Tires Facebook and Instagram accounts. The application does not sell personal information, run third-party advertising profiles, or share access tokens with outside parties.

Data Storage

Configuration values and access tokens are stored in private environment variables controlled by Rebel Wheels and Tires. Generated promotional images may be uploaded to a storage bucket so Meta can retrieve them for publishing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent data. Data is shared only with service providers required to operate the workflow, such as Meta APIs for publishing posts and storage providers used to host generated images.

Data Deletion

To revoke this application's access, remove the app from your Meta Business or Facebook account settings, or remove the relevant access tokens from the application's environment configuration. Stored generated images may also be deleted from the configured storage bucket.

Contact

For privacy questions or access removal requests, contact Rebel Wheels and Tires through its official Facebook Page or Instagram account.
